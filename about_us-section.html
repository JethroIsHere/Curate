Bridging Canvas and Conversation

Traditional gallery tours leave you with unasked questions. "curate." changes how you experience art history by introducing an interactive, intelligent AI Docent. Instead of just reading static plaques, our platform invites you to ask critical, natural questions about the masterpieces you view.

Powered by Natural Language Processing, our Docent dynamically generates context-aware, historically accurate answers, giving you a personalized, conversational journey through the world's greatest artworks.
